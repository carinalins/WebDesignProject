ACOMODATION

Where you choose to live is a very important decision of your study abroad experience. At ICOT, we have different types of accommodation options to suit your lifestyle, budget and needs.

Host Family

In a host family you will live with native English speakers, and you will have the chance to experience an authentic Irish lifestyle. You will live as your hosts live, eat what they eat and spend time at home together.

Speak English everyday

Living with an Irish family will provide you the opportunity to speak English in a very natural way every single day.

Cultural experience

Get involved with the local culture and experience Irish traditions day-to-day family life: activities, holiday celebrations and local cuisine.

Quiet environment

Have a relaxing atmosphere at home. Living with your host family will give you tranquility and a quiet and peaceful place for relaxation and study.

Hostel and Student accommodation

That is the option for those who want to live with students from all around the world. If you would like to stay in a residence with other English learners, our range of accommodation options ensures you find the perfect home away from home.

Be independent

Living with other students means you have more freedom. It’s a great option if you enjoy your independence and privacy.

Make new friends

Meet loads of people in a short time. Have the chance to live with people from different nationalities and learn a bit more about several different cultures.

Fun atmosphere

Staying with other students means that you can practice your English all the time while talking about everything. Hostels and student accommodations usually have lively atmospheres suitable for anyone who loves socialising.

HEALTH INSURANCE

It is a requirement of their Study Visa for non-EU students to take out private health insurance. For non-EU students who do not have current Health Insurance arrangements in place, ICOT will arrange student medical cover with O’Driscoll O’Neil. These policies cover emergency medical expenses, emergency travel and repatriation expenses and are compliant with the applicable visa regulations.

LEARNER PROTECTION

In 2015, ICOT College announced one more measure that will assure protection for the current and future students: Learner Protection. With this implementation, the student who hires a course with ICOT College will be under the support of insurance, which will come into effect immediately if the contracted services can not be provided by the institution. For educational purposes, Learner Protection includes a policy and contract that ensures reimbursement to the student if the school can not offer the course in which he/she is enrolled. Insurance is available now and can be purchased by students doing short and long term courses. In addition to Learner Protection, ICOT College is a member of the Private College Network (PCN) – an organization of private schools that have a common interest in promoting English language teaching in Ireland and abroad and improving education standards and respect within this branch.

PAY TO STUDY

PaytoStudy offers a fast and secure way to send payments globally at no cost to the payer or the payee by using a simple 3 step process.

1. Register at icot.paytostudy.com

The sender registers on the secure PaytoStudy domain and enters their payment details.

2. Make payment in local Account

The sender receives the exchange rate for that day (valid for 48 hours) and makes payment into the PaytoStudy bank account in their home country and in their local currency.

3. PaytoStudy makes payment to school

Once funds are received by PaytoStudy, payment is immediately transferred to the receivers account from the local PaytoStudy account in their home country.
